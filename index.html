Reality Construction for Thinking Things

By Ronald Morris Sborz Jr. — Expanded Edition — ronsborzmind.com

Abstract

This book traces a single continuous path from the breaking of symmetry 13.8 billion years ago to the question of how you know when you are wrong. Reality is constructed through traceable chains of emergence governed by one structural principle: variation under constraint, maintained by feedback, viable only at the boundary between rigidity and dissolution. Master principle: every system that stays open to correction survives; every system that closes, dies.
